We Kill Deer When Appropriate

Ticks depend on deer to survive in their second year of life. They are also over populated in most of the United States. In regions with high deer-tick populations, it is guaranteed there are high amounts of deer!

We are not talking about Bambi here. We are talking about large, strong, fast, and overfed deer who continue to over-populate because of climate, environmental, and predator-less eco-systems.

We have a trained team of deer hunters with crossbows who literally kill as many deer as they are allowed to in a safe and sustainable fashion.
